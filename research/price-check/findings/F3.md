# F3: Spanish construction cost databases

## Findings

### [1] Standard full renovation (reforma integral) in Spain ranges €600-1,200/m² in 2025, with economy options from €350-500/m²
- quote: "En 2025, el precio medio de una reforma integral oscila entre 600€ y 1.200€ por metro cuadrado, dependiendo de múltiples factores"
- url: https://presupuestoreforma.com/precio-reforma-integral/
- source_type: secondary (renovation marketplace/guide)
- published: Oct 28, 2025
- confidence: medium

### [2] For a 70m² apartment, standard renovation costs €35,000-56,000; economy starts at €21,000; premium from €56,000-105,000
- quote: "Para un piso de 70m², entre 21.000-35.000€ [básica]; entre 35.000-56.000€ [estándar]; desde 56.000€ hasta 105.000€ o más [premium]"
- url: https://presupuestoreforma.com/precio-reforma-integral/
- source_type: secondary (renovation marketplace/guide)
- published: Oct 28, 2025
- confidence: medium

### [3] Economy renovation starts at €350-500/m²; standard at €600-800/m²; premium at €900-1,300/m²; luxury at €1,400-2,200/m² — based on 847 completed reforms
- quote: "Reforma Básica: 350-500€... Reforma Estándar: 600-800€... Reforma Premium: 900-1.300€... Reforma Lujo: 1.400-2.200€"
- url: https://datosfinancieros.es/2025/12/30/coste-medio-de-reforma-integral-por-m2/
- source_type: secondary (financial analysis site)
- published: Dec 30, 2025
- confidence: medium

### [4] Madrid/Barcelona add +5% over base renovation prices; Canarias/Baleares add +8-10%; rural areas -5%
- quote: "Madrid / Barcelona: +5 % sobre la tabla base... Islas (Canarias y Baleares): +8-10 % por transporte de materiales"
- url: https://domdiseno.com/precio-reforma-integral-2025-tabla/
- source_type: secondary (design/renovation guide)
- published: Jul 3, 2025
- confidence: low

### [5] ACR Construction Cost Index reached 185.42 points in 2025 (+5.46% YoY), with plumbing/sanitation up +8.28% and earthworks +18.75%
- quote: "Los costes directos de construcción en edificaci\u00f3n residencial... cerraron 2025 con una subida del 5,46%, seg\u00fan el \u00cdndice de Costes Directos de Construcci\u00f3n de ACR, que se situ\u00f3 en 185,42 puntos"
- url: https://forbes.es/economia/880305/los-costes-de-construccion-subieron-un-5-5-en-2025-por-la-escasez-de-profesionales-y-la-inflacion/
- source_type: secondary (Forbes España reporting on ACR primary index)
- published: Feb 26, 2026
- confidence: high

### [6] MITMA official construction cost index shows average residential construction at ~1,323€/m² (new build), +3.76% YoY as of Q3 2025
- quote: "Coste de Construcción Vivienda Nueva: 1.323 €/m² (septiembre 2025), +3,76% (interanual)"
- url: https://estimationqs.com/costes-de-construccion-por-metro-cuadrado-en-espana/
- source_type: secondary (aggregating INE/MITMA official data)
- published: Oct 2025
- confidence: high

### [7] IVE (Instituto Valenciano de la Edificación) publishes the official Base de Datos de Construcción covering all 17 autonomous communities, with parametric unit prices for rehabilitation work
- quote: "La Base de Datos de Construcción se actualiza en un contexto de incremento generalizado de los costes de construcción, con especial incidencia en la mano de obra, los materiales estructurales, los bituminosos..."
- url: https://www.five.es/costes-de-construccion
- source_type: primary (government-backed research institute)
- published: 2026 edition
- confidence: high

### [8] INE housing price index (IPV) shows average free-market housing at €1,959-2,047/m² in Q2-Q3 2025, +6.9-10% YoY; new build specifically at €2,528/m²
- quote: "Precio Medio Vivienda Libre (Nueva y Usada): 1.959-2.047 €/m² (Q2-Q3 2025), +6,9% a +10,01%"
- url: https://estimationqs.com/costes-de-construccion-por-metro-cuadrado-en-espana/
- source_type: secondary (aggregating INE primary data)
- published: Q3 2025
- confidence: high

### [9] Labor costs in Spanish construction run ~25-30€/hour, up 5.4% YoY (ICLA INE), with skilled worker shortage driving costs up; workforce age averaging 45 years
- quote: "Coste Laboral por Hora Efectiva (Construcción): ~25-30 €/hora (estimado Q2 2025), +5,4% (ICLA INE)"
- url: https://estimationqs.com/costes-de-construccion-por-metro-cuadrado-en-espana/
- source_type: secondary (aggregating INE ICLA data)
- published: 2025
- confidence: high

### [10] MITMA Índice de Costes del Sector de la Construcción (ICSC) — official government index now transitioning to base year 2021, published quarterly by the Ministerio de Transportes
- quote: "una vez superado el tercer año desde el año 2021, los Índices de Costes del Sector de la Construcción pasarán a publicarse en base 2021"
- url: https://search.yahoo.com/search?p=ICIO+indice+costes+edificacion+reforma+pisos+precio+m2
- source_type: primary (Spanish government — Ministerio de Transportes)
- published: 2025
- confidence: high

### [11] Substructure costs in renovation: demolition ~121€/m², installations (plumbing+electric) ~192€/m², masonry/finishes ~272€/m² — from real 95m² Valencia renovation case
- quote: "Demolición y preparación: 8.500€ (121€/m²)... Instalaciones (fontanería + electricidad): 18.200€ (192€/m²)... Albañilería y acabados: 25.800€ (272€/m²)"
- url: https://datosfinancieros.es/2025/12/30/coste-medio-de-reforma-integral-por-m2/
- source_type: secondary (financial analysis with case study)
- published: Dec 30, 2025
- confidence: medium
