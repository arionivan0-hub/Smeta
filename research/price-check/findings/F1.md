## Assessment vs. €262/m² (€18,314.50 for 70 m²)

€262/m² falls **far below** even the lowest end of habitissimo's ranges:
- Reformar piso page: minimum 450 €/m² (range 450-700)
- Reforma integral page: minimum 400 €/m² for "calidades medias-bajas" (range 400-1,200)
- For 70 m² specifically: minimum 28,000€ (reforma integral) or 30,000€ (reformar piso)

€18,314 for 70 m² represents roughly **37-43%** of the low-end estimate. This would only be plausible for a very partial renovation (reforma parcial ~200 €/m² on a subset of the apartment), not a full integral reform. Even a partial renovation of the full 70 m² at 200 €/m² = 14,000€ is cheaper, but that excludes most structural work, installations, and finishings.
