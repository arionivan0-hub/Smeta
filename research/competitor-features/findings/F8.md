# F8: Habitissimo

## Findings

### [1] Habitissimo is fundamentally a lead-generation marketplace, not a budgeting or cost-estimation tool
- quote: "Cuéntanos qué necesitas y contacta gratis con pintores, carpinteros, empresas de reformas, mudanzas y más..."
- url: https://www.habitissimo.es/
- source_type: primary
- published: unknown (site active since 2009)
- confidence: high

### [2] Habitissimo has processed 6,490,630+ budget requests and lists 490,429+ professionals on its platform, with 2+ million monthly visits
- quote: "6.490.630 presupuestos solicitados... 490.429 profesionales... +2 millones de visitas al mes"
- url: https://www.habitissimo.es/calculadora-profesionales
- source_type: primary
- published: unknown
- confidence: high

### [3] The "Guía de precios" (Price Guide) is editorial SEO content with aggregated real-data prices, not an interactive budget calculator
- quote: "Los precios que aparecen en esta Guía de Precios proceden de información real que recibimos de nuestros usuarios particulares y profesionales, contrastada y revisada por expertos del sector"
- url: https://www.habitissimo.es/presupuestos/reformas-banos
- source_type: primary
- published: 10/6/2026 (article update date)
- confidence: high

### [4] Price guides provide average prices, price ranges, and city-level breakdowns, but no itemized budget builder or export functionality
- quote: "Precio medio 3000 € — Rango de precios 1200 € - 4500 €"
- url: https://www.habitissimo.es/presupuestos/reformas-banos
- source_type: primary
- published: 10/6/2026
- confidence: high

### [5] The only "calculator" is a professional ROI/investment calculator, not a construction budget calculator
- quote: "Calcula la Rentabilidad de trabajos y proyectos... Conoce rápidamente tus ganancias mediante tu inversión en habitissimo"
- url: https://www.habitissimo.es/calculadora-profesionales
- source_type: primary
- published: unknown
- confidence: high

### [6] The professional ROI calculator lets users select trade category, average job value, and desired number of extra projects to compute estimated revenue vs. lead cost
- quote: "¿Qué trabajos realizas principalmente? — ¿Cuánto sueles facturar por cada trabajo que realizas? — ¿Cuántos proyectos extra necesitas al año?"
- url: https://www.habitissimo.es/calculadora-profesionales
- source_type: primary
- published: unknown
- confidence: high

### [7] Professionals manage a "weekly budget" (presupuesto semanal) that caps weekly spending on lead purchases with automatic Monday recharges
- quote: "Tu presupuesto semanal te permite indicar el importe máximo que deseas dedicar cada semana a la compra de contactos... Cada lunes se realizará una recarga automática"
- url: https://soporte.habitissimo.com/hc/es/articles/8340706213148
- source_type: primary
- published: 4 January 2024
- confidence: high

### [8] Habitissimo offers a mobile app for professionals only (not for homeowners), focused on lead management and client contact
- quote: "habitissimo, is the platform chosen by thousands of people to find a professional like you... Our application will allow you to access budget requests for works and reforms"
- url: https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.habitissimo.app.pro
- source_type: primary
- published: Updated May 25, 2026 (app listing)
- confidence: high

### [9] The professional mobile app has 500K+ Google Play downloads but only a 3.4-star rating, indicating significant user dissatisfaction
- quote: "3.4 — 4.49K reviews — 500K+ Downloads"
- url: https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.habitissimo.app.pro
- source_type: primary
- published: unknown
- confidence: high

### [10] Homeowner workflow is a simple 3-step process: submit project details, receive up to 4 professional contacts, discuss and hire — no budgeting tool is offered
- quote: "1. Nos cuentas tu proyecto de reformas baños. 2. Compara los mejores profesionales y empresas de reformas baños. 3. Podrás obtener presupuestos y contratar, si lo deseas, al profesional que más te guste."
- url: https://www.habitissimo.es/presupuestos/reformas-banos
- source_type: primary
- published: unknown
- confidence: high

### [11] Price guides include itemized cost breakdowns by work category (e.g., desescombro 600 EUR, fontanería 600 EUR, electricidad 900 EUR for bathroom reform) but these are static editorial, not calculable
- quote: "Mano de obra... entre un 10% y un 20% del presupuesto final — Desescombro... alrededor de 600 € — Instalación de fontanería... alrededor de 600 € — Instalación eléctrica... aproximado de 500 €"
- url: https://www.habitissimo.es/presupuestos/reformas-banos
- source_type: primary
- published: unknown
- confidence: high

### [12] Habitissimo operates in 4 countries (Spain, Portugal, Chile, Mexico) and publishes sector reports via its Procenter hub, but offers no API, integration, or collaboration features
- quote: "España... Portugal... Chile... México" (site footer country links); Procenter offers "Informe anual sobre el sector de la reforma en España"
- url: https://www.habitissimo.es/procenter/
- source_type: primary
- published: unknown
- confidence: high

## Dead ends
- habitissimo.es/empresa/ returned 404 — no standalone "empresa" landing page exists
- habitissimo.es/app/ returned 404 — app download is at /landing/app-movil-pro/descarga
- No evidence of PDF/Excel/CSV export of budgets or price data
- No evidence of any interactive construction budget calculator for end users (homeowners)
- No evidence of API, third-party integrations, or collaboration features
- No iOS app listing was found (only Google Play for Android)

## Suggested follow-ups
- Does Habitissimo offer any premium tier for professionals that includes budget/invoice generation tools?
- Are there any Habitissimo API endpoints or embedded widgets used by partner sites?
- Does the iOS version of the professional app have different features than the Android version?
