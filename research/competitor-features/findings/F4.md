# F4: PlanSwift

## Findings

### [1] PlanSwift is a Windows desktop application requiring Windows 10/11 Pro or Enterprise (64-bit recommended), with no cloud or mobile version available.
- quote: ""Desktop-based. Requires a Windows environment. Cloud access is not currently available.""
- url: https://www.planswift.com/estimating-software/planswift-vs-stack/
- source_type: primary
- published: unknown
- confidence: high

### [2] PlanSwift's Takeoff Boost AI suite (launched May 2026) performs actual takeoff measurements: Auto Takeoff generates area/linear/count in under a minute; Auto Count detects repeated symbols across up to 10 pages in 1-2 minutes.
- quote: ""Auto Takeoff generates a full-page takeoff draft (area, linear, count) in under a minute and Auto Count detects and counts repeated symbols across up to 10 pages in 1-2 minutes.""
- url: https://www.planswift.com/estimating-software/planswift-vs-stack/
- source_type: primary
- published: unknown
- confidence: high

- quote: ""PlanSwift's 18+ trade assembly libraries let you drag an assembly onto a takeoff and watch the cost calculate in real time. Custom formulas let you build your trade logic into the tool.""

- source_type: primary
- published: unknown
