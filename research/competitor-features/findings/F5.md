# F5: Buildxact

## Findings

### [1] Buildxact is a cloud-based all-in-one construction estimating and job management platform targeting residential builders, renovators, and trades across UK, Australia, US, Canada, and New Zealand
- quote: "ALL-IN-ONE SOFTWARE FOR CONSTRUCTION PROS Ś Run your construction projects, from takeoff to invoice"
- url: https://www.buildxact.com/uk/
- source_type: primary
- published: unknown (current website)
- confidence: high

### [2] Three-tier pricing model (Foundation/Pro/Master) with unlimited users on all plans; annual pricing ranges from GBP 89/mo to GBP 262/mo with 15% annual discount
- quote: "Foundation GBP 119/mo GBP 89/mo | Pro GBP 209/mo GBP 160/mo | Master GBP 349/mo GBP 262/mo"
- url: https://www.buildxact.com/uk/pricing/
- source_type: primary
- published: unknown (current pricing)
- confidence: high

### [3] Core budgeting features include: digital takeoffs from PDF plans (80% faster claim), estimating with templates and live supplier pricing, customizable quote letters with digital signatures, purchase orders, work orders, cost tracking by job/category/item, change orders, and timesheets
- quote: "Complete takeoffs online up to 80% faster Ś No more paper plans and scale rulers ¢ upload the PDF and do your takeoffs on-screen, in minutes"
- url: https://www.buildxact.com/uk/features/
- source_type: primary
- published: unknown
- confidence: high

### [4] AI-powered Blu assistant suite with three tools: Takeoff Assistant (auto-scales, auto-measures, names pages), Estimate Generator (creates full estimates from project description using local pricing data), and Estimate Reviewer (checks for compliance, missed items, insurance coverage)
- quote: "Blu: Takeoff Assistant Ś Blu auto names and scales plan pages so you get right to work. With AI and other Buildxact features, you cut the time to measure and count materials by 65%"
- url: https://www.buildxact.com/uk/blu-ai-construction-assistant/
- source_type: primary
- published: 2025/2026 (Blu feature pages reference 2026 badges)
- confidence: high
