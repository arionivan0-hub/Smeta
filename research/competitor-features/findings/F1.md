# F1: Presto (Grupo Precon)

> **Important note**: Research reveals that Presto is **not** made by "Grupo Precon." Presto is developed by **RIB Spain**, which was acquired by **GRAITEC** (via acquisition of the Autodesk and RIB Presto business unit from Cad&Lan). "Grupo Precon" (precon.es) is an unrelated German weight-loss products company. The product is now sold as "GRAITEC Presto" or "RIB (Presto)" under the GRAITEC umbrella. The company behind Presto's development is **RIB Software**, originally a Spanish firm.

## Dead ends
- "Grupo Precon" does not exist as a construction software company; precon.es is a German diet/weight loss products company. The research brief's association of Presto with "Grupo Precon" appears to be incorrect.
- RIB Spain's own website (rib-software.es) returned HTTP 410 (Gone), suggesting the domain has been migrated or decommissioned following GRAITEC's acquisition.
- DuckDuckGo and Bing search engines blocked automated queries after initial searches (CAPTCHA/bot detection), limiting the breadth of search.
